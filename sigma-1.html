What is Sigma dashboard?

What is Sigma dashboard?



Summary

Sigma Computing is a cloud-native analytics platform that provides business users with instant access to explore and gain insights from their cloud data warehouse. Its familiar spreadsheet interface allows users to interact with data without requiring any code or specialized training. With Sigma, you can analyze and visualize live marketing data, unencumbered by the size limitations of traditional spreadsheets.

In 2023, I was introduced to Sigma Computing, a data company that offers a new generation of BI (Business Intelligence) tools that work perfectly as Tableau alternative. Since then, I worked on moving companies of different sizes from different BI tools (Tableau, Looker, etc.) to Sigma to improve their dashboard experiences for their marketing teams.

Sigma provides spreadsheet interface dashboard that allows users to interact with data without requiring any code or specialized training. Here's how it can help marketing programs in any-sized companies.

  • Campaign Dashboard: Sigma enables marketing analysts and campaign managers to create customized dashboards. These dashboards serve as a central hub for monitoring campaign performance. From here, you can track key metrics, visualize trends, and make data-driven decisions.
  • Drilling into Data: Sigma allows you to drill down from the campaign dashboard to the detailed, underlying data. This capability is crucial for performing calculations, analyzing leads, bookings, and understanding how different customer segments convert along the sales pipeline1.
  • First Touch Analysis: By tracking lead generation and conversion rates, you can gain insights into the effectiveness of your initial touchpoints with potential customers.
  • Campaign Analysis: Explore key metrics using predefined filters. Understand how different campaigns are performing in terms of leads, bookings, and conversions.
  • Visual Campaign Dashboard: Monitor lead, contact, and conversion trends over time. Visualizations help you identify patterns and adjust your strategies accordingly.
