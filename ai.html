Main Concepts of Artificial Intelligence

Although we hear the word "Artificial Intelligence" or "AI" so often these days, what it exactly means is not recognized by a lot of people. (at least I was one of them.) So I looked up what AI means and found that it consists of many different concepts and ideas that computers can do as humans do, or even better in some areas. Here is a 5-minute video that explains different branches of AI very well.


As mentioned in the video,

  • There are two ways AI works, simbolic-based and data-based (Machine Learning).
  • Machine Learning requires lots of data to feed the machine.
  • There are different ways to use machine learning, categorized as supervised learning and unsupervised learning.

A lot of these ideas can be used to create a tool that gets some tasks done extremely faster and better than before. One of the examples is this company that integrate AI-based machine translation with human translators to make translation more affordable and efficient. Visit their website to learn how they do this.
